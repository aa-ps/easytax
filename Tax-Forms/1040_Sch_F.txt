The 1040 Schedule F to report Farm income and expenses. This tax for can be attached to the 1040, 1040-SR, 1040-SS, 1040-NN 1041 and 1065Farm income comes from selling livestock due to weather-related conditions though you need to meet few conditions such as farming being your main business, show proof of livestock being sold do to weather-related issues, and the area you farm qualifies for federal aid. 

Nondeductable Farming Expenses

1) Expenses that don't bring income to the farm.

2) Expenses that you and the family will use, such as raising livestock or produce that will be consumed by you.

3) The value of animals you raised that have died.

4) Inventory losses.

5) Personal losses.
